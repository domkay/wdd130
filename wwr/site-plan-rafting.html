Overview

Purpose

The rafting site aims to attract thrill-seekers as well as those who love the great outdoors. (beginners as well as professionals) by offering/presenting different rafting's sites.

Audience

- Who are our target customers? sports enthusiasts, regardless of their age, who seek thrilling moments in nature

- What do they want? They are looking for a rafting site where they can practice this activity according to their level of rafting.

- What do they expect to find? A catalogue of rafting sites.

- How will my audience usually access my site? Any device that has a web browser.
